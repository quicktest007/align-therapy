How it works

Get started in three simple steps. We make it easy to find the right fit.

1. Book a consult

Schedule a free 15-minute call. We'll learn about your goals and match you with a licensed therapist who specializes in your needs.

2. Meet your therapist

Connect via secure video. Your first session is a chance to build rapport and create a personalized plan together.

3. Build lasting change

Work at your own pace with evidence-based techniques. Track progress and adjust as you grow.
